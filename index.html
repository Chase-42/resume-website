How We Help

Save The Animals is a crowd-sourcing application for Animal Conservation. We have a two-pronged approach to this. First, we find conservation projects that are aligned to the user interests and concerns and connect them. Secondly, we approach the issue from the opposite side and allow conservation groups to broadcast their outreach to users and accept donations.
